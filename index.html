Why Hybrid over Native apps?

Pros

  • Save time and money
  • Give web developers the ability to write apps

Cons

  • Instant boot time
  • Specific goals (e.g. video games)

Why Ionic over competitors?

Pros

  • Community
  • Moving (super) fast
  • Free and open source
  • Web technology, flexible GUI
  • Lot of libs and plugins
  • Work closely with other tech

Cons

  • I don't like Angular
  • Versatility of the (Cordova) plugins
